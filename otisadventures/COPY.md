## Place Descriptions (Northern Ireland 2025)

### Giant's Causeway
Those hexagonal stones are absolutely magic — Otis is going to love clambering all over them! Go early to beat the crowds.

### The Dark Hedges
A magical tunnel of ancient beech trees. Free, quick, and absolutely beautiful. Best early morning for photos.

### W5 Science Centre
So many interactive exhibits — you could spend a whole day here. Perfect for curious little minds! Book online in advance.

### Ulster Museum
Free entry! Dinosaurs, mummies, animals — Otis is going to go wild. Botanic Gardens right outside. Closed Mondays.

### Armagh Planetarium
Space shows made for little ones. Has a brilliant sensory room too. Check for toddler-specific shows. Closed Mondays.

### Carrickfergus Castle
Right on our doorstep! A proper medieval castle Otis can explore. Some areas under renovation — check before you go. Closed Mondays.

### Castle Ward
800+ acres of National Trust magic. Adventure play area, farmyard, and stunning Strangford Lough views.

### Whiterocks Beach
White chalk cliffs and beautiful rock pools. Pair with Giant's Causeway nearby for a full north coast day!

### Portstewart Strand
NI's best beach — you can drive onto the sand! National Trust. Perfect and safe for toddlers.

### Drum Manor Forest Park
Butterfly garden, forest trails, duck pond and a great play park. A lovely picnic spot. £5 car parking.

### Murlough Nature Reserve
Stunning sand dunes with the Mourne Mountains as a backdrop. National Trust. Go at low tide — check before you go!

### Crawfordsburn Country Park
Beach, waterfall and woodland all in one. Free entry, very close to home, and a brilliant café at the visitor centre.

### Belfast Zoo
Lions, giraffes and so much more up on Cave Hill. Great play park on site too. Wear comfy shoes — it's built on a hill!

### Exploris Aquarium
Seal sanctuary and aquarium in Portaferry. Touch tanks, penguin feeding and a soft play area. Check feeding times before you go!

### WWT Castle Espie
Ducks literally come right up to you! Woodland play area, soft play and a lovely café on Strangford Lough.

### Glenariff Forest Park
A fairy-tale forest with magical waterfalls. Take the waterfall trail — it's the toddler-friendly route. Café on site.

### Seaforde Butterfly House
Tropical butterflies flying all around you and peacocks wandering the gardens. Pure magic for little ones.

### Ark Open Farm
Rated 4.8★ by everyone who visits! Hands-on with animals including reptiles. Amazing café food too.

### Streamvale Open Farm
Otis' home favourite! Sheepdog demos, tractor rides and a deer herd. Opens 10:30am. Book ahead at busy times.

### Play Factore
Massive soft play — the biggest in NI! Perfect for a rainy day. Call ahead to check the toddler area is open.
